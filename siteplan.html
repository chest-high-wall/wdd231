Site Plan: RC Crawlers Southern Utah

Site Name

RC Crawlers Southern Utah – This name reflects the hobby focus on rock crawlers and trail RC vehicles, specifically highlighting Southern Utah’s unique terrain and trails. It’s niche, descriptive, and location-specific.

Optional Domain: rccrawlerssouthernutah.com

Site Purpose

This website serves as a central hub for RC rock crawler enthusiasts in Southern Utah. The site will showcase trail locations, vehicle mod recommendations, and user-submitted RC rigs. It includes interactive guides, upgrade breakdowns, and a form to submit trail reports or RC builds. Designed to be responsive, fast, and easy to use.

Scenarios

  • What are the best trail RC spots in Southern Utah for crawling?
  • Which upgrades make the biggest difference for a stock SCX24 crawler?

Color Schema

The site will use dark greens and earthy tones to reflect an outdoor, mechanical theme.

  • #2a5d3b – Used for headings, nav background, and emphasis.
  • #6db181 – Used for buttons, accents, and hover states.

Typography

  • Playfair Display – Used for headings to give a rugged, outdoor brand feel.
  • Roboto – Used for body text to improve legibility and clarity.
